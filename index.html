These 3 fonts have different feelings, with Croissant One being the classic cursive, and Raleway / Overpass both being sans-serif fonts.

I think Croissant One suits the titles and main headings quite nicely, since they're large and easy to read even in cursive.

I also think that Overpass can substitute for a minor headings and links on the navbar.

Raleway is one of my favourite fonts to use for text just because of how nice it looks.
